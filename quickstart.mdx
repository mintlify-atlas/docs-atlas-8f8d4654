## Overview

This guide will walk you through accessing the platform, creating an account, and completing your first forum activity.

<Note>
  If you're a professor, use the admin account (`admin@gmail.com`) to access the professor panel instead.
</Note>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Platform">
    Navigate to the platform URL provided by your instructor. You'll see the login page with two panels:
    
    - Left panel: Decorative welcome screen with "Portal de Derivadas"
    - Right panel: Login/registration form
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    If you don't have an account yet:
    
    1. Click **"Regístrate aquí"** (Register here) at the bottom of the form
    2. Fill in your information:
       - **Nombre** (First name)
       - **Apellidos** (Last name)
       - **Correo Electrónico** (Email address)
       - **Contraseña** (Password)
    3. Click the **"Registrarse"** button
    4. Wait for the confirmation message: "Cuenta creada, Ahora inicia sesión"
    
    <Info>
      Your email will be used as your unique identifier throughout the platform.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In">
    Once registered (or if you already have an account):
    
    1. Enter your **email** and **password**
    2. Click **"Entrar"** (Enter)
    3. You'll see a success message and be redirected automatically
    
    <Tip>
      You can press **Enter** after typing your password to submit the form quickly.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View the Welcome Screen">
    After logging in for the first time, you'll see a welcome message:
    
    - Personalized greeting with your first name
    - Explanation of the platform's purpose: understanding derivatives through bone density analysis
    - The relationship between age and bone mineral density (BMD)
    
    Click **"Comenzar"** (Begin) to start your learning journey.
    
    <Note>
      On subsequent logins, you'll skip this welcome screen and go directly to Forum 1.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Forum 1">
    After clicking "Comenzar", you'll be taken to **Forum 1: La densidad mineral ósea** (Bone Mineral Density).
    
    The sidebar on the left shows all available activities:
    - Foro 1 through Foro 6
    - Examen 1 and Examen 2
    - Panel de Alumno (Student Panel)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the Educational Material">
    Before answering questions, review the provided material:
    
    1. Click **"Mostrar Material"** to reveal:
       - Graph showing bone density vs. age
       - Explanation of what BMD is (mg/cm²)
       - Information about bone composition (collagen, calcium phosphate)
       - Details about DEXA/DEX testing
    
    <Info>
      The material explains that bone density increases from birth to adulthood, then begins declining after age 35.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Forum 1 Questions">
    Forum 1 contains 6 questions about bone mineral density:
    
    1. **Question 1**: Explain what BMD is and how it's measured
    2. **Question 2**: What factors influence good bone health?
    3. **Question 3**: Are age and BMD related?
    4. **Question 4**: How does BMD evolve throughout life?
    5. **Question 5**: Can you determine age from BMD and vice versa?
    6. **Question 6**: Is BMD the same for male and female skeletons?
    
    Type your answers in the text areas provided.
    
    <Tip>
      You must complete at least the first two questions to submit the form.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit Your Responses">
    When you're ready:
    
    1. Click the **"Enviar Respuestas"** (Submit Responses) button at the bottom
    2. You'll see "Guardando..." (Saving...) while your responses are sent to the server
    3. Once saved, you'll see a success confirmation
    
    <Warning>
      You can only submit each forum **once**. Make sure your answers are complete before submitting!
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Peer Responses">
    After submitting, the form transforms into a discussion view showing:
    
    - All classmates' responses with their names and submission times
    - Avatar circles with initials
    - Expandable details to read full responses
    - A green "Actividad Completada" (Activity Completed) banner
    
    This allows you to learn from your peers' perspectives and insights.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Continue to Next Forum">
    Use the sidebar to navigate to the next forum:
    
    1. Click **"Foro 2"** in the left sidebar
    2. Repeat the process: review material, answer questions, submit
    3. Continue through all 6 forums and 2 exams
    
    <Info>
      Your professor can provide feedback on your submissions through the **Panel de Alumno** (Student Panel).
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features

### Student Panel

Access your personal dashboard by clicking **"Panel de Alumno"** in the sidebar:

- View your progress across all forums and exams
- See feedback from your professor on specific activities
- Track which activities you've completed

### Visual Learning (Forum 5)

Forum 5 includes special features for visual analysis:

- Upload sketch images for question 3
- Upload up to 3 images for the data table
- View peers' uploaded images after submission

### Responsive Design

The platform works on all devices:

- Desktop: Full sidebar navigation always visible
- Mobile: Collapsed menu for compact screens
- Dark mode support for comfortable viewing
